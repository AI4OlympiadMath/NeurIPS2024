Introduction

AI for Olympiad Math is a recent research frontier and includes all work leading to AI solving International Math Olympiad level problems.
We will have invited speakers, presentation of reviewed / accepted papers, and considering this is such a new area, a panel of experts to allow for discussion and audience participation. We will also organize a mini-competition for submitted systems before the workshop and start the benchmarking site.
